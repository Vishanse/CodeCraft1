Understanding <h> and <p> Tags in HTML

In HTML, the <h> tags (like <h1> to <h6>) are used to define headings. These tags help structure your webpage content, with <h1> being the most important heading and <h6> the least. Proper use of heading tags improves accessibility and SEO, which is essential for AdSense approval.

The <p> tag defines a paragraph. It is used to display blocks of text and is a fundamental element for organizing written content on your webpage. Well-structured paragraphs improve user experience and content clarity.
